Probability Distribution Calculation

Health Score = (Price Change × 0.3) + (Inverse Volatility × 0.3) + (Inverse PE × 0.2) + (Dividend × 0.2)

Confidence = 60% + (Data Completeness × 40%)

Risk Metrics Formulas

  • Liquidity: min((volume / avgVolume) × 50 + 50, 100)
