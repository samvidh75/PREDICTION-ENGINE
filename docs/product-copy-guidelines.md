# Product Copy Guidelines

## Core Principle
StockStory is the AI research layer between Indian investors and brokers. Copy should sell the product, not apologize for it. Legal protections must exist but should not dominate the user experience.

## Value-Led Copy Principles
- Lead with what the user can do, not what the product doesn't do
- Answer: What is this? Who is it for? What problem does it solve? What do I do first?
- Use action-oriented language: "Start research", "Open scanner", "Compare companies"
- Avoid defensive framing: "Research only", "Not advice", "Not a broker" as headlines
- Avoid fear-based copy on main marketing pages

## Legal Placement Rules
### Allowed locations for legal/disclaimer copy:
- Footer fine print
- Terms & Disclosures page
- Research Standards page (relevant sections)
- Invest handoff compact disclaimer
- About page compact legal line

### Forbidden locations:
- Hero blocks
- Feature cards
- Auth marketing panels
- Dashboard cards
- Repeated badges
- Primary CTAs

## Forbidden Marketing Claims
- Guaranteed returns
- Price targets
- Buy/Sell/Hold recommendations
- "Trusted by thousands"
- "Award-winning"
- "Number one platform"
- Broker partner claims (unless verified)
- SEBI registration claims (unless verified in repo config)
- Fake testimonials
- Fake user counts
- Fake broker logos

## Allowed Research Labels
- High conviction research case
- Worth researching
- Track
- Needs review
- Risk rising
- Avoid for now
- Research signals pending

## Forbidden Labels
- Buy, Sell, Hold, Strong Buy, Strong Sell
- Target price, Guaranteed upside
- Multibagger, Sure shot, Profit guaranteed
- "best stock to buy", "AI picks", "Top picks"
- Try Pro, Unlock Pro, Trade now, 30 days free

## CTA Naming Rules
### One primary CTA per major section
- Primary: filled/colored button, highest visual weight
- Secondary: outlined/ghost button
- Legal/help links: text links, fine print

### CTA labels
- Sign in / Sign up / Create free account (auth)
- Start research / Open scanner / Search company (discovery)
- Research / Compare / Track / Review risks (actions)
- Invest review / Continue to broker (handoff)
- Read Research Standards / Terms & Disclosures (support)

### Avoid:
- Icon-only main actions
- Dead buttons
- Repeated "Methodology" as primary action
- "Learn more" as primary (be specific)

## Auth Page Copy Rules
### Sign-up panel:
- Title: "Build your Indian equity research workspace."
- Bullets: research rankings, company research, compare, track thesis, broker handoff
- Compact legal line: "StockStory provides informational research tools. Read Terms & Disclosures."

### Sign-in panel:
- Title: "Pick up where your research left off."
- Bullets: saved research, watchlists, compare, portfolio thesis, broker handoff
- Compact legal line: "StockStory provides informational research tools. Read Terms & Disclosures."

## Empty State Copy Rules
- Explain what the section does
- Provide clear next action
- No backend/provider wording
- No fake data
- No giant empty panels with no direction

### Examples:
- Scanner empty: "No results found. Adjust your filters or try a different search."
- Watchlist empty: "Track companies from search or company pages to monitor changes."
- Portfolio empty: "No thesis tracked yet. Add positions to monitor companies you are researching."
- Dashboard empty: Guided start panel with suggested workflow cards.

## Broker Handoff Copy Rules
- "Final order will be placed with your broker."
- "No broker credentials are stored in StockStory. No order has been placed."
- No fake broker connection or sync
- No fake order preview values
- No Buy/Sell/Hold labels
- No price targets
- Explain as informational review flow

## Backend/Provider Wording
### Never show in product UI:
- Provider names (IndianAPI, Yahoo, Jugaad, etc.)
- API names, provider health, provider unreachable
- Source labels, data coverage, lineage
- Migrations, backfills, diagnostics
- Backend error, API error, HTTP status codes
- Raw exceptions, raw JSON
- Prediction registry

### Allowed product-safe fallback language:
- "Research signals pending"
- "Needs research"
- "Awaiting research cycle"
- "Track this company to review changes over time"
- "Search a company to begin research"
- "Compare before investing"
- "Review risk before continuing"
