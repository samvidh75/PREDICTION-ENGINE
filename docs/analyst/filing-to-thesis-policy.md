# Filing-to-Thesis Policy

## Materiality labels

- `high_materiality` — financial results, major corporate actions
- `medium_materiality` — governance-sensitive disclosures
- `low_materiality` — routine announcements
- `informational` — noted but limited thesis impact
- `insufficient_information` — no filing content available

## Rules

- Do not invent filing content
- Use "may affect", "needs review", "limited information"
- High materiality + low confidence → human review
- Governance claims require evidence

## Outputs

- Filing brief, materiality score, thesis impact, risk impact, affected factors
- Suggested follow-up task (earnings_review, risk_review, filing_review)

## Public safety

- No parser/source internals in user-facing copy
