# Research Q&A Policy

## Supported question types

- Company overview, thesis, risk, valuation, earnings, peer, sector, filing, scenario, methodology

## Unsupported / advice requests

Questions like "Should I buy/sell?" are classified as `unsupported_or_advice_request` and redirected to:

- Thesis and risk research framing
- Compare and methodology guidance
- Explicit "not investment advice" disclaimer

## Evidence requirements

- Factual claims must link to evidence IDs where possible
- Numeric claims must match evidence
- Insufficient information → clear limitation message

## Forbidden outputs

- Buy/Sell/Hold recommendations
- Price targets
- Guaranteed returns
- Personal financial advice

## Public surface

- Shows: concise answer, Research basis, limitations, confidence
- Does not show: internal source IDs, provider payloads, audit trail
