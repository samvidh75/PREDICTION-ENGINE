# StockStory Intelligence Calibration Changelog

## Part 8 — Market Reality Calibration & QA (2025)

### Overview

This changelog documents all calibration changes made during the Part 8 validation and market reality calibration phase. Each change is traced to the validation finding that triggered it, the module affected, and the expected impact.

---

## Calibration Changes

### 1. Sector Calibration Framework (Phase 6)
- **What**: Created `CalibrationTypes.ts` with 7 sector profiles and 4 market-cap buckets
- **Why**: Previous scoring treated all sectors identically, ignoring Indian market structural differences
- **Impact**: Sector-aware PE/PB ranges, key metric selection, and factor weight adjustments

### 2. Market Cap Tiering (Phase 7)
- **What**: Added 4-cap-tier growth expectations and liquidity discounts
- **Why**: Mega-caps grow differently than small-caps; liquidity affects execution risk
- **Impact**: Growth projections and scoring now vary by cap size

### 3. Evidence Bounding (Phase 8)
- **What**: Minimum evidence thresholds, staleness checks, source diversity requirements
- **Why**: High-conviction outputs with 1-2 stale data points were misleading users
- **Impact**: Outputs flagged as "insufficient data" when evidence is thin

### 4. Risk Calibration (Phase 9)
- **What**: India-specific risk-free rate (7.0%), equity risk premium (5.5%), country premium (1.5%)
- **Why**: Global defaults (US 10Y at 4%) don't apply to Indian equities
- **Impact**: More accurate WACC, DCF valuations, and risk scoring

### 5. Contradiction Detection (Phase 10-11)
- **What**: Cross-field contradiction engine (thesis vs. risk, valuation vs. quality, etc.)
- **Why**: Systems generating "high conviction" + "high risk" simultaneously erode trust
- **Impact**: Contradictory outputs flagged for human review

### 6. Explainability QA (Phase 12)
- **What**: Explanation completeness, readability, evidence-alignment, and language checks
- **Why**: One-line explanations without context don't help users make informed decisions
- **Impact**: Short/unexplained outputs get flagged; forbidden language caught

### 7. Ranking Sanity (Phase 5, 13)
- **What**: 7 sanity checks on top-position rankings (risk, confidence, debt, traps)
- **Why**: High-risk/low-confidence symbols appearing in top-10 erodes ranking credibility
- **Impact**: Sanity issues surfaced for review before rankings published

### 8. Forbidden Language Safety (Phase 21)
- **What**: Automated grep for investment advice phrases, backend noise, fake data, US symbols
- **Why**: "Buy now", "multibagger", GPU/Ollama references leak internal infrastructure
- **Impact**: CI pipeline can now block forbidden language at build time
