# Moat Analysis — StockStory vs Competitors

## Competitive Landscape

### Screener.in
- **Strength:** Best Indian fundamentals
- **Weakness:** No predictions, no forward analysis
- **StockStory Advantage:** StockStory adds predictions, alpha, narratives

### Tickertape
- **Strength:** Good UI/UX, basic screening
- **Weakness:** Limited deep analysis
- **StockStory Advantage:** StockStory adds quality V4, risk decomposition, narratives

### Trendlyne
- **Strength:** Institutional tracking
- **Weakness:** Limited fundamental depth
- **StockStory Advantage:** StockStory adds factor engine V2, future health

### Moneycontrol
- **Strength:** Massive reach, news
- **Weakness:** No predictive analytics
- **StockStory Advantage:** StockStory adds prediction engine, alpha tracking

### TradingView
- **Strength:** Charting, global community
- **Weakness:** Limited Indian fundamentals
- **StockStory Advantage:** StockStory adds Indian-specific quality model

- Live prediction engine with alpha certification (TRACK-45)
- Multi-horizon outcomes (7d/30d/90d/180d/365d)
- Factor Engine V2 in production
- Chain of custody for every prediction
- Full explainability for every score
- Future health projections (3m/6m/12m)
- Narrative intelligence
- Institutional confidence tracking
- SEBI-compliant educational framing
- Data truth certification
