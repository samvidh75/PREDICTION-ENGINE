# AGENT B — Stock Compare Tool

## Implementation
- **File**: src/components/company/StockCompare.tsx
- **Pattern**: Side-by-side comparison with 9 categories

## Features
1. ✅ Input two symbols with quick presets
2. ✅ Health Score comparison with bar charts
3. ✅ Quality, Growth, Stability, Valuation, Risk, Momentum
4. ✅ Future Health 3M/12M comparison
5. ✅ Prediction Hit Rate comparison (when available)
6. ✅ Winner per category (no overall recommendation)
7. ✅ Narratives displayed for both companies

## Competition Analysis
- **Screener.in**: Allows comparison but limited factor analytics
- **Tickertape**: Good comparison UI but limited future projections
- **Trendlyne**: Strong on technical but weak on fundamental comparison
- **StockStory advantage**: 7-engine factor comparison + narrative + predictions

## SEBI Compliance
- "Winner by category" NOT "buy/sell recommendation"
- No investment advice language
- Disclaimer present
