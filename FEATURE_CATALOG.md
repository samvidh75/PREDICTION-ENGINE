# FEATURE CATALOG

This catalog documents the 10 core technical and statistical features generated by **StockStory Feature Engine v1**. All features are normalized, mathematically defined, and stored in the `feature_snapshots` table of the PostgreSQL warehouse.

---

### 1. RSI (Relative Strength Index)
*   **Database Column**: `rsi`
*   **Description**: Momentum oscillator that measures the speed and change of price movements between 0 and 100.
*   **Parameters**: Period = 14 days
*   **Formula**:
    $$RSI = 100 - \frac{100}{1 + RS}$$
    where $RS = \text{Wilder's Smoothed Average Gain} / \text{Wilder's Smoothed Average Loss}$.

### 2. MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)
*   **Database Column**: `macd`, `macd_signal`, `macd_histogram`
*   **Description**: Trend-following momentum indicator showing the relationship between two moving averages of prices.
*   **Parameters**: Fast Period = 12 days, Slow Period = 26 days, Signal Period = 9 days
*   **Formula**:
    $$\text{MACD Line} = \text{EMA}_{12}(\text{Close}) - \text{EMA}_{26}(\text{Close})$$
    $$\text{Signal Line} = \text{EMA}_{9}(\text{MACD Line})$$
    $$\text{Histogram} = \text{MACD Line} - \text{Signal Line}$$

### 3. ADX (Average Directional Index)
*   **Database Column**: `adx`
*   **Description**: Directional movement index that quantifies trend strength regardless of direction.
*   **Parameters**: Period = 14 days
*   **Formula**:
    $$ADX = 14\text{-day Smoothed Average of } DX$$
    where $DX = 100 \times \frac{|+DI - -DI|}{+DI + -DI}$.

### 4. ATR (Average True Range)
*   **Database Column**: `atr`
*   **Description**: Indicator measuring market volatility by decomposing the entire range of an asset price for that period.
*   **Parameters**: Period = 14 days
*   **Formula**:
    $$\text{ATR}_t = \frac{\text{ATR}_{t-1} \times 13 + \text{TR}_t}{14}$$
    where $\text{TR}_t = \max(\text{High}_t - \text{Low}_t, |\text{High}_t - \text{Close}_{t-1}|, |\text{Low}_t - \text{Close}_{t-1}|)$.

### 5. Bollinger Width
*   **Database Column**: `bollinger_width`
*   **Description**: The width of the standard Bollinger Bands relative to the middle moving average, measuring volatility squeeze.
*   **Parameters**: Period = 20 days, StdDev multiplier = 2
*   **Formula**:
    $$\text{Bollinger Width} = \frac{\text{Upper Band} - \text{Lower Band}}{\text{Middle Band}}$$
    where $\text{Middle Band} = \text{SMA}_{20}(\text{Close})$ and $\text{Bands} = \text{Middle Band} \pm 2 \times \sigma_{20}$.

### 6. Momentum
*   **Database Column**: `momentum`
*   **Description**: Percentage rate of price change over a 10-day lookback window.
*   **Parameters**: Lookback = 10 days
*   **Formula**:
    $$\text{Momentum}_t = \frac{\text{Close}_t - \text{Close}_{t-10}}{\text{Close}_{t-10}}$$

### 7. Volatility
*   **Database Column**: `volatility`
*   **Description**: Annualized historical daily returns standard deviation over a rolling 20-day window.
*   **Parameters**: Window = 20 days
*   **Formula**:
    $$\text{Volatility} = \text{StdDev}(\text{Daily Returns}) \times \sqrt{252}$$

### 8. Relative Strength
*   **Database Column**: `relative_strength`
*   **Description**: The difference between the asset's return and the average return of the entire market universe for that date.
*   **Formula**:
    $$\text{Relative Strength}_t = \text{Return}_{asset, t} - \text{Average Return}_{market, t}$$

### 9. Moving Average Distance
*   **Database Column**: `moving_average_distance`
*   **Description**: The percentage distance between the current close price and its 50-day Simple Moving Average.
*   **Parameters**: Period = 50 days
*   **Formula**:
    $$\text{MA Distance} = \frac{\text{Close}_t - \text{SMA}_{50}(\text{Close})}{\text{SMA}_{50}(\text{Close})}$$

### 10. Trend Strength
*   **Database Column**: `trend_strength`
*   **Description**: A compound index assessing the trend using the normalized distance between short-term (20-day) and long-term (50-day) EMA, weighted by ADX.
*   **Parameters**: Short EMA = 20, Long EMA = 50
*   **Formula**:
    $$\text{Trend Strength}_t = \frac{\text{EMA}_{20} - \text{EMA}_{50}}{\text{Close}} \times \left(1 + \frac{\text{ADX}}{100}\right)$$
