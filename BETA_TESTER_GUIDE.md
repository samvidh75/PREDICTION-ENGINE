# Beta Tester Guide

Welcome to the StockStory Closed Beta! Follow this structured guide to explore the platform and record feedback.

---

## Testing Workflow

### Task 1: Create an Account
1. Navigate to the landing page.
2. Click the **Get Started** button.
3. Fill in a valid email address and password, then click **Sign Up**.
4. Confirm you are redirected onto the dashboard page.

### Task 2: Sign Out & Log In
1. In the navigation rail, click **Logout**.
2. Click the **Sign In** button.
3. Sign in using the credentials you created.
4. Confirm you are returned back to your dashboard.

### Task 3: Search for a Company
1. Locate the search bar at the top of your dashboard.
2. Type `INFY` or `RELIANCE` and select the symbol from the results dropdown.
3. Verify you navigate to the Booklet page for that company.

### Task 4: Read Company Intelligence
1. Scroll down through the 11 booklet sections (Hero, Executive Summary, The Story, Business/Financial Quality, Ownership, Valuation, Risks, Catalysts, Timeline, Peers).
2. Move your cursor across the interactive canvas chart and verify crosshair hover trackers render prices smoothly.

### Task 5: Create a Watchlist
1. Click **Watchlist** in the navigation sidebar.
2. Click **Create New Watchlist**, input a name (e.g. "My Growth Stocks"), and click Save.
3. Add a few symbols (like `INFY`, `HAL`) to the list.

### Task 6: Create a Portfolio
1. Click **Portfolio** in the navigation sidebar.
2. Verify you can allocate weight values to your symbols.
3. Click Save and confirm portfolio factor metrics are calculated.

### Task 7: Create an Alert
1. Trigger a test notification or review listed alerts in the alerts centre.
2. Click **Mark Read** on an active alert and verify it transitions into archived logs.

### Task 8: Session Persistence Check
1. Refresh your browser window.
2. Confirm you remain logged in and your portfolio allocations and watchlists persist without errors.
