# About Page Specification

The entry point of StockStory India (`/` or `page=about`) is a clear, narrative About page designed to explain the product inside 15 seconds. It completely replaces sci-fi telemetry aesthetics with direct financial utility.

## Content & Layout Specification

### 1. Hero Section
* **Headline:** "Understand Indian companies."
* **Subheadline:** "StockStory transforms complex financial data into understandable company intelligence. No noise, no advice—just clean, structural clarity."
* **Primary CTA:** "Enter Platform" (Redirects to `/login`)
* **Secondary CTA:** "Create Free Account" (Redirects to `/signup`)

### 2. What StockStory Does
* **Core Philosophy:** Explain that StockStory is not a stock screener or trading platform. It is an explanatory research layer.
* **Three Pillars:**
  1. **Discover opportunities:** Find stocks based on underlying factor shifts (not generic screens).
  2. **Understand companies:** Read direct, research-grade narratives on corporate financials.
  3. **Monitor changes:** Get concise summaries of ownership shifts and valuation compressions.

### 3. Intelligence Engine Visual
* Renders a clean structural flowchart showing how data flows from sources to investor insights:
  ```
  [ Financial Filings & Shareholding PDF Reports ]
                        │
                        ▼
            [ Structural Feature Engine ]
                        │
                        ▼
             [ Valuation & Quality Factors ]
                        │
                        ▼
         [ Understandable Company Intelligence ]
  ```

### 4. Example Company Analysis
* Display a mock or interactive high-fidelity card for a company (e.g., **Reliance Industries**):
  - **Quality Score:** 83/100
  - **Valuation:** Undervalued
  - **Ownership:** Mutual funds increased stake by 0.8% during retail EBITDA acceleration.
  - **Financial Summary:** A simple, 50-word narrative explaining that strong retail performance offsets refining margin pressures.

### 5. Why Investors Use It
* **Zero Hype:** Focuses entirely on evidence-based data.
* **Non-Discretionary:** No stock recommendations or financial advice.
* **Time Saving:** Summarizes long-form reports and numbers in seconds.

### 6. CTA & Footer
* A minimal, clean bottom section prompting the user to log in or create an account.
* Simple footer with privacy policy, terms, and SEBI compliance notes.
